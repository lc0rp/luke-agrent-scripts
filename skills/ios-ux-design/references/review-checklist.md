# Review Checklist

Use this before handoff.

## Native Feel

- Does the screen feel like iOS at first glance?
- Is the shell appropriate for the task?
- Does anything feel like a website squeezed into a phone?

## Structure

- Is the primary task obvious?
- Is navigation shallow enough?
- Is the chosen pattern simpler than the alternatives?

## Touch Ergonomics

- Are touch targets at least 44x44pt?
- Are primary actions easy to reach?
- Are destructive actions explicit?

## Typography And Symbols

- Are text styles semantic and readable?
- Are SF Symbols used appropriately?
- Is hierarchy clear without decorative excess?

## Visual System

- Are semantic colors used where appropriate?
- Does dark mode hold up?
- Are materials, corners, and shadows restrained?

## States

- Loading state present?
- Empty state present?
- Error state present?
- Disabled and confirmation states present where needed?
- Permission or offline states handled if relevant?

## Accessibility

- Dynamic Type works?
- VoiceOver labels and order make sense?
- Contrast acceptable?
- Reduced motion respected?

## Stress Cases

- Longer localized strings survive?
- Large content sizes survive?
- Small phones still work?

## Final Question

- Would a strong iOS designer or engineer believe this was designed for the platform on purpose?
